How We Deploy

You change nothing. We do everything.

No migrations. No new EHR. No IT project. We integrate alongside your existing systems and operate the platform so your team only sees the results.

1

Discovery & integration mapping

We confirm your EHR, phone system, and SMS provider during the discovery call. Our integration library covers Athena, eClinicalWorks, Epic, NextGen, DrChrono, and the major ambulatory EHRs via native API or RPA bridge.

2

Configuration & voice tuning

We configure the agents to your practice's workflows, scripts, and tone of voice. The voice agent learns how your front desk actually answers the phone — same warmth, same brand, same handoff patterns.

3

Soft launch & supervised mode

Agents start in supervised mode — humans review every interaction for the first two weeks. Once accuracy clears our quality bar, the agents go fully autonomous on the workflows they're cleared for.

4

Operate & optimize

We run the platform. You see the results in Practice BI and a monthly business review. New workflows roll out as the practice needs them — no change orders, no new contracts.
