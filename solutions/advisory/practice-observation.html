What's Included

A half-day on-site.
A report that earns its keep.

Fixed scope. Fixed price. You know exactly what you're getting — and when the report lands in your inbox.

Full-practice on-site observation

We arrive at 8 AM and stay through the end of morning sessions. We sit with the front desk, walk the exam rooms, listen to inbound calls, watch check-ins, observe checkouts, and follow the morning's billing workflow. Your team barely notices us after the first 30 minutes.

Six-touchpoint diagnostic

Every touchpoint is scored against benchmark independent-practice performance. We don't grade — we map where the work is leaking, flag the leading indicators, and surface what we couldn't observe directly so you can instrument it yourself.

Written findings report in 10 business days

Executive summary, touchpoint-by-touchpoint findings, prioritized recommendations sized in dollars, and a 12-month ROI projection. Roughly 25 pages of substance. Yours in PDF and editable formats — to act on with or without us.

Readout call

A 90-minute working session with the practice owner and operational lead. We walk through findings, answer hard questions, and align on what would move first if you choose to act. The next step is always yours.

The Discipline
"Most operators already sense where the leaks are. The observation surfaces the ones they don't — the ones no one would ever flag in a meeting because they've become invisible."
The Engagement

Three weeks. End to end.

1

Week 1 · Discovery + scheduling

A 15-minute call confirms fit and scope. We schedule the on-site day. You share your EHR vendor, phone system, and any recent performance reports you have — for context, not for grading. We arrive with fresh eyes.

2

Week 2 · On-site observation day

Half-day at your practice. We arrive at 8 AM, leave at lunch. We cover all six touchpoints — phones, scheduling, check-in, exam room flow, checkout, and billing handoff. Light footprint: structured notes, nothing disruptive to your team or your patients.

3

Weeks 2–3 · Report drafting

We synthesize the observations into the written report, score the touchpoints, prioritize the findings, and size the revenue impact of each gap. Roughly 70–80 hours of work before the report goes back to you.

4

Week 3 · Report delivery + readout

The report lands on day 10. We schedule a 90-minute readout within the same week — usually with the owner and ops lead. After that, the next step is yours: act on the report independently, or engage us to implement.
