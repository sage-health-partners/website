A 90-day roadmap from where you are to where you want to be.

The Three Phases

Assess. Implement. Optimize.

After the Evaluation Framework, the next question is always the same: "Where do we start?" The Strategic Transformation engagement answers it across 90 days, with concrete deliverables at every checkpoint.

Month 01
Assess

Baseline current state across the five pillars. Lock the KPIs you want to move. Align the team on priorities and quick-win initiatives that launch in week two. By month-end, the team knows what's changing and why.

Month 02
Implement

Roll out core workflow changes. Deploy SOPs across the team. Activate the technology where the assessment called for it — including the Practice Agentic Suite, if that's the right move. KPIs start to move in the dashboard.

Month 03
Optimize

Measure against the month-one baseline. Refine the workflows that need it. Lock the quarterly review cadence so the gains compound. Hand over the operating playbook to your team — or continue running it together.

What's Included

Four deliverables. Sized in dollars.

Every initiative is named, scoped, and tied to a measurable KPI. The roadmap is a working document — not a binder that gets shelved after the kickoff meeting.

Implementation Roadmap

Initiatives sequenced by effort and impact across 12 weeks, with clear milestones for every two-week sprint. Visual Gantt + written narrative. Updated weekly.

Technology Recommendations

Aligned to your existing EHR and phone stack. No forced replacements. Where the Practice Agentic Suite fits, it's named; where another vendor fits better, we say that too.

Revenue Quantification

Projected financial impact per initiative — sized in dollars, with confidence ranges, not aspirational headlines. Tracked against actuals month by month.

Quarterly Business Reviews

Built-in checkpoints to track progress, surface friction, and adjust priorities as the practice changes. The first one comes built into the engagement; subsequent reviews are part of the ongoing partnership.

The Accountability
"Recommendations are where this engagement starts, not where it ends. The 90 days are about getting the work done — with monthly check-ins, named owners, and KPIs that have to move."
Who It's For

Practices ready to move.

Strategic Transformation is a real engagement — it requires a practice owner who will hold their team accountable and a front-office lead who can execute. We're a force multiplier, not a substitute for leadership.

Best fit

Two-to-twenty-physician independent practices in primary care, internal medicine, pediatrics, family medicine, OB/GYN, or low-complexity specialty. Practice owner is actively involved. Front-office lead has authority to execute.

Not for everyone

If you're looking for a binder of recommendations to file, this isn't it. If you're a multi-hundred-physician system with a strategy office of your own, we're probably the wrong partner. If you want execution and accountability — we're built for that.
