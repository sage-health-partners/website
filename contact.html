Common Questions

Before the call.

Does the discovery call cost anything? +

No. The 15-minute call is complimentary and carries no obligation. Most calls end with one of three outcomes: schedule the assessment, schedule a follow-up after you've talked internally, or part ways with you having a clearer picture of your options.

Do you work with practices outside of Texas? +

Yes. Our home markets are Houston and Dallas, but our on-site assessments travel. For practices outside the four-hour drive radius, we pass through travel at cost — no markup.

Do I have to use both Advisory and Technology? +

No. Most practices start with the Operations Assessment — our Front Desk Evaluation Framework applied on-site — and use the findings on their own. Others come straight to us for the Practice Agentic Suite. Active Partnership is the ongoing engagement that combines both: we deploy the Practice Agentic Suite, refine SOPs, and operate alongside you with monthly accountability reviews. That's a choice you make after the assessment — not a precondition.

Will the agents replace my front-desk staff? +

No. The agents handle the repetitive, after-hours, and overflow work that's currently falling on the floor — calls that go unanswered, confirmations that don't get sent, eligibility checks that get skipped. Your staff is freed up to do the higher-value patient work that requires a person.

Does the Practice Agentic Suite work with my EHR? +

Almost certainly. We integrate with Athena, eClinicalWorks, Epic, NextGen, DrChrono, and the major ambulatory EHRs via either native API or an RPA bridge. The discovery call is the right place to confirm.

What does this cost? +

It depends on scope. The Operations Assessment is a fixed engagement priced to the size of your practice. The Practice Agentic Suite is a monthly per-practice subscription. We share specifics on the discovery call — once we understand what you're actually trying to solve.
