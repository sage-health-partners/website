How we work

These principles guide how we operate so we can help you serve your community.

Evidence before opinion.

We observe your practice onsite and collect real data so we can surface real opportunities to you. We don't like recommending generic tools.

Implement, not just recommend.

We're ready to implement what we recommend so you can focus on taking care of patients.

Customer-owned outputs.

Any deliverables like SOPs, roadmaps, etc are yours to keep after our partnership ends.

Independent practice, by name.

We serve independently owned practices because their work is what built American healthcare and the vendor class has stopped showing up for them.

Healthcare tech experts with an eye for operations and great patient experiences.

We bring a combined 20+ years of healthcare tech experience from places like AWS, CVS and McKesson. We believe the most direct way to impact healthcare is to serve practices holistically, not through a small slice of software. Our focus is independent practices with 1–3 locations. We believe in serving our local community in Houston and Dallas.
