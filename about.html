Our Story

Operational methodology, purpose-built for the independent practice.

We've spent our careers building operational systems at scale — the kind of infrastructure that obsesses over phone metrics, eligibility verification, and the unglamorous work that keeps the revenue moving. We've seen what works.

What we found, repeatedly, is that independent practices face the same problems the big systems face — unanswered calls, no-shows, missed eligibility, copays that walk out the door — but without the back-office headcount to solve them. The methodology exists. The technology exists. They just haven't met the independent practice in a useful way.

That's the gap Sage Health fills. We bring the assessment frameworks, the SOPs, and the agentic technology that previously required a six-figure consulting engagement and an IT department. We deliver it on-site, in plain language, with the agents already configured for your EHR.

We are operators. We've sat at the desks we now help operate. Every recommendation we make has either worked or failed for one of us, in a real practice, on a real Tuesday.

We work across primary care, pediatrics, optometry, dental, OB-GYN, and multi-specialty groups — adapting our methodology and technology to each specialty's unique operational demands. See how we adapt to your specialty

How We Work

Four principles. Non-negotiable.

We don't have a long list of values. We have four operating principles — and we'd rather walk away from work that violates them than do it anyway.

Evidence before opinion.

Every recommendation is grounded in observed data — call logs, no-show rates, the actual numbers from your actual EHR. We don't generalize from someone else's case study. If we don't have the data, we say so.

Operator-led, always.

The Sage Health partnership includes operating the technology we deploy. Our incentives stay aligned with your outcomes, every month — long after the assessment is delivered, while the work is being done.

Customer-owned outputs.

The Operations Assessment report is yours. The SOPs are yours. The training materials are yours. If our partnership ends, you keep the playbook. We've earned the work back if we want it again.

Independent practice, by name.

We don't serve hospital systems. We don't serve private-equity-rolled-up MSOs. We serve independently-owned practices because their work is the work that built American healthcare — and because the vendor class has stopped showing up for them.
